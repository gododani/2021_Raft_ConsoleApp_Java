This game is made as a Console Application.

A) To RUN: 1) Open IntelliJ 
	   2) Open Raft folder as project.
	   3) Run the Main.java

B) Informations:

	- Character: 		1
	- Shark:		S
	- Water: 		-
	- Platform (raft):	0
	- Cooker:		C
	- Water purifier:	W
	- Fishing net:		X
	- Plank:		P
	- Leaf:			L
	- Junk:			J
	- Barrel:		B

To cook and eat you have to move to the spot of the cooker when it is ready.
To drink you have to move to the spot of the water purifier when it is ready.
